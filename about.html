About this image

In an outdoor sketch in 2018, I drew many characters on this kraft paper sketch book, from which I accidentally extracted a bald image with glasses.After that, I refined and summarized the image of this bald figure, making it more concise and interesting.I try to use this image as the main element in the picture, create a series of hand-drawn cartoons in the form of diaries, and record and express my life in a more interesting way.

Of course, the image of bald head does not refer to a certain person or a certain kind of person, but only represents the species. The people or lives I have observed are becoming more and more numb or similar, which is why I will describe people very similar in the picture.
